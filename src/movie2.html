American Sniper is a story about Navy SEAL sniper Chris Kyle. His father teaches him how to shoot deers when he was little, and he becomes a cow boy after years.

He meets a girl at a bar one night and married her. One day he sees the news about the war so he decides to be a sniper.

He is a great sniper and he is considered as a hero after he is sent to the war several times. But when he gets back from the war he realizes that his family is falling aprt.

After long efforts, he finally becomes a good father and a great husband, but he is killed accidentally by an old man.

He had a great funeral and is memorized by people til now.
