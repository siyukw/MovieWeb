Tiny time is a serie of movies. Up til now, there are three one.

It is mainly a story about four high school girls. They are best friends since high school, and they are going to the same college.

But things changed after they graduated from college. They are working against each other and their friendship is breaking apart. Also, their different family backgrounds are adding oil to the fire.

After Lee's father dies, everything is going down. They are having more troubles in their life, and some of them stopped being friends.They became loners in the world.

At the end of the last one, a fight takes place between two girls, one of them is injured badly. Where is life leading them to? Are they able to achieve their dream?

We don't know. Because it's life, and it's unpredicable.
